Capital Programs Analytics Infrastructure Redesign

SQL Power BI Data Modeling Vena (FP&A)

Executive Summary

Led the overhaul of the capital programs' data and analytics environment, transitioning from siloed, manual data sources to a centralized, governed data warehouse solution. The initiative established standardized financial reporting, enabled real-time budget forecasting in Vena, and deployed self-service Power BI dashboards, resulting in significant time savings and improved capital allocation decision-making across the firm.

The Challenge: Data Silos and Reporting Lag

The Capital Programs department managed a large, multi-million dollar budget, but critical operational and financial data were fragmented across various manual trackers and legacy systems. This structure led to several key pain points:

  • **Inaccurate Forecasting:** Budget forecasting was time-intensive and error-prone, requiring weekly manual consolidation and reconciliation in Microsoft Excel.
  • **Lack of Real-Time Visibility:** Senior leadership lacked immediate insight into project status, budget burn rates, and renovation tracking, hindering timely capital allocation decisions.
  • **Data Governance:** Discrepancies between operational data and financial data required significant manual auditing time (estimated at 5-10 hours per week).

The Solution: Establishing a Single Source of Truth

I designed and implemented a comprehensive solution focused on data centralization and automation:

  • **SQL Data Warehouse:** Developed robust **SQL** scripts and views to cleanse, standardize, and load data from multiple source systems (including renovation, job tracking, and financial ledgers) into a central data warehouse. This established a single source of truth for all Capital Program metrics.
  • **Vena Integration:** Designed the data modeling, templates, and training materials for the Capital department’s budgeting and forecasting workstream in **Vena** (a corporate FP&A platform). This replaced Excel-based models and enabled dynamic, real-time budget forecasting tied directly to the central data warehouse.
  • **Power BI Automation:** Automated data auditing processes and deployed over **7 Power BI reports** covering portfolio metrics, budget vs. actual performance, and renovation progress, providing self-service analytics to 500+ monthly users.

Results and Business Impact

The implementation delivered tangible results by enhancing efficiency and providing strategic insights:

  • **100% Real-Time Reporting:** Eliminated reporting lag, enabling management to make highly accurate, data-driven decisions on capital allocation and project oversight.
  • **Significant Time Savings:** Reduced manual data auditing and reconciliation efforts by an estimated **80%**, freeing up analyst time for value-added analysis rather than data cleanup.
  • **Improved Data Quality:** Automated data auditing processes resulted in a sustained improvement in data governance and trust in key operational metrics.
  • **Enhanced User Adoption:** Successful deployment of interactive reports and the Vena system cemented my role as the organizational SME for Capital data strategy.
